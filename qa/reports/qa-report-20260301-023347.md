### 🟠 High

- **Sidebar wayfinding lost** — icon-only rail; nav items no longer labeled. A new user can't tell which icon is "Ring Queue." Previous labeled sidebar was more legible for demos. (Trade-off: space gained, discoverability lost.)
- **Risk scores lack visual encoding** — colored numbers only; no badge containers or spark-bars to give scores visual weight.
- **Status badge colors too saturated** — teal (NEW) and blue (REFERRED) are close in hue; could confuse at a glance.
- **No sort affordances beyond EXPOSURE** — MEMBERS, RISK, DETECTED appear non-sortable or at least unsignaled.

### 🟢 Low
- **Blue dot at bottom-left of sidebar** — unexplained artifact, present since baseline.
- **KPI area needs card containers** — free-floating numbers lack visual separation.
